iOS APP：Coffee It - Track Caffeine in Simplest Way

发表 2017-03-20   |   更新 2017-03-28 | 分类于 iOS Develop（iOS 开发） | | 本文被观赏 次

Coffee It is an iOS APP that build for those who drink Coffee a lot. There are group of people who want to log all Caffeine they drink each time. And from all iOS APPs, most Caffeine Tracker APP are been abandoned in a few time, without further bug fixes and improvement, the APP are difficult to use. This is why I develop Coffee It.

I’m a student in Computer Science, and I learn Swift and iOS Development myself. This APP may not fulfill every Caffeine Lover’s requirement, but I will keep developing it with any feedback! I cannot assure I can do every function since I still need time for my University Study, but with my spare time, I will do my best in improving it.
